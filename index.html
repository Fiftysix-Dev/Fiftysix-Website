the world is our blank canvas

Fiftysix is a social enterprise. We take the most powerful consumer electronics that run the future of the 21st Century, and reproduce it to a design that allows kids to build it from the ground up, tinker with it and intuitively understand what makes it go.

  • What we do

Our Tablets

Snap each piece together, lock each part in and power up. It’s like LEGO for the 21st Century. And, by purchasing one tablet, you're contributing to the education of a child in a disadvantaged community.

Our Workshops

Our workshops prepare kids for the real world. Creativity, computer science and entrepreneurship are all covered in our workshops.

Education at Fiftysix

As of December 2016, we've educated 45,209 kids. And we're not stopping anytime soon. Together, we're committed to educating 1,000,000 kids by 2020.

Our Team

The people in our team are constantly learning and innovating to inspire the leaders of the future. It's what we do.
