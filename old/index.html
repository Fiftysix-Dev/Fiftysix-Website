Fiftysix Education

A build-it-yourself tablet & coding kit for kids. As easy as a puzzle, as fun as a computer game.

The World Is Our Blank Canvas

Fiftysix is a social enterprise. We take the most powerful consumer electronics that run the future of the 21st Century, and reproduce it to a design that allows kids to build it from the ground up, tinker with it and intuitively understand what makes it go.

Signature Hardware Design

For a new genre of hardware.

Young people cannot simply play with and consume technology, but must learn to create and build it. We’ve developed a method by which you can build your very own tablet computer, along the way learning about what really makes it go.
